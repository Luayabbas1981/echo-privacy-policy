# Privacy Policy for Echo

Last updated: June 2026

Echo is an independent mobile puzzle game developed by Luay Abbas.

The game does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or home addresses.

## Advertising

Echo uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information required for advertising and service operation, including:

* Device information
* Advertising identifiers
* IP address
* Basic usage and diagnostic information

Google processes this information according to its own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Usage

Data collected by third-party services may be used for:

* Displaying advertisements
* Improving advertisement quality
* Maintaining application functionality
* Diagnosing technical issues

## Children's Privacy

Echo is not specifically designed for children under the age of 13.

## Third-Party Services

Echo currently uses:

* Google AdMob

Third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.
